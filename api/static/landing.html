Why Analytiq

What the upload-a-CSV tools can't do

Most AI dashboard tools are cloud apps you paste a spreadsheet into. Analytiq is built for companies that need their data to stay private, their answers to be verifiable, and their live systems connected.

Runs on your own infrastructure

Cloud, your private cloud, or fully air-gapped on-prem. On managed cloud your dataset stays in your store — only the schema and query results reach the model. On-prem, the model runs on your hardware and nothing leaves your network at all.

Shows its work

Every answer carries the tables it used, the exact SQL it ran, and a flag when retrieval is uncertain. Auditable by design — no black-box “insights.”

Connects to your live database

Read-only connections to Postgres, MySQL, SQL Server, and warehouses — answers reflect today's data, not a stale export. Files and documents too.

Speaks your business

Define your metrics once and every answer uses your definitions, not a generic guess at a spreadsheet.

net_revenue = sales − returns
How it works

From a question to a verified answer

Three steps. Every answer comes back with the receipts to check it.

01 · Connect

Connect your data

Point Analytiq at your database with a read-only role, drop in files, or upload a spreadsheet. It reads the schema automatically.

02 · Ask

Ask in plain English

Analytiq retrieves the relevant tables, writes the SQL, and pulls in supporting documents for the “why.”

“Show net revenue by quarter and explain the dip.”
03 · Verify

Get an answer that proves itself

A chart, a written explanation, and the receipts — the tables and SQL used, plus a confidence flag — so you can trust it or check it.

Deployment

Your data, your rules

One product, two ways to run it. Choose based on how your data has to be handled — not on what a vendor will allow.

Cloud

Up in minutes, managed for you

  • Per-customer isolation and access keys
  • Connect a database or upload files
  • Managed model API — your dataset stays in your store; only query results are sent
  • Best for teams without a compliance constraint
On-premise

Your hardware, fully air-gapped

  • Open-source model runs on your hardware
  • Works fully air-gapped — no internet required
  • Connect directly to your internal database
  • Built for regulated and data-sovereign teams
